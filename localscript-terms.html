LocalScript — Terms of Use

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

1. About the App

LocalScript is a privacy-first voice transcription app for iPhone. All audio recording and transcription occurs entirely on your device using the Whisper Tiny Core ML model. No data is transmitted to any server.

2. Subscription

LocalScript offers an optional Pro subscription:

  • Monthly: $6.99/month
  • Annual: $59.00/year (save 29%)

Subscriptions are auto-renewable and managed through Apple App Store. You can cancel anytime via iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

3. Free Tier

The free tier includes up to 10 minutes of recording per session with basic functionality.

4. Data & Privacy

LocalScript does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. All voice recordings and transcripts remain on your device at all times. See our Privacy Policy for details.

5. Disclaimer

LocalScript is not intended for professional, medical, legal, or clinical decision making. It is a simple personal note-taking assistant.
